LAUFEY

Laufey Lín Jónsdóttir is a rising star in the contemporary jazz scene who has taken the genre by storm with her unique approach to blending jazz and classical music. The Icelandic-Chinese artist's musical journey began with the cello and piano before transitioning to jazz in her teens. Her first two full-length debut albums, "Everything I Know About Love" and "Bewitched" have broken streaming records and topped jazz charts, introducing a new generation to jazz through her social media platforms. Laufey's music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, and coming-of-age experiences, resonating with young listeners while paying homage to jazz traditions. In 2023, Laufey won a Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album, solidifying her status as a groundbreaking artist.

With her classical training, jazz influences, and modern approach to music-making and promotion, Laufey's experience is at the forefront of a new wave of artists bringing jazz to a broader, younger audience; and as someone who was brought into the jazz space through her songs, I can confirm this as truth.
